Methodology & Caveats

How this list was built: Programs were scored using publicly available directory information, known agency partnerships, geographic/climate data, and institutional reputation. The ranking is personalized to one specific applicant profile (described above) and is not a universal quality ranking.

Where uncertainty is highest: Many directory-only programs lack detailed public descriptions. Scores for these programs rely on proxies: historically known EMS systems, partner agency presence, air-medical affiliations, and geography. Programs with limited public information are noted.

This is a starting point, not a final answer. Visit programs, talk to current fellows, and weight your own priorities. Your mileage will vary.
